A similar phenomenon occurs with the mice's temperature.

There seems to be a wide range of data even on a daily basis.

98.6ºF or 37ºC is a healthy human body temperature.
The mouse equivalent is 98.4ºF or 36.95ºC!

BUT mice have higher body temperatures at night than during the day.

AND female mice typically have higher body temperatures than male mice.

THEREFORE a single temperature reading clearly does not tell the whole story of mouse health.
